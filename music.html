Songs of Queen

Works of Queen

Queen is known for making empowering, catchy music that can be pleasing to our ear and even to our brains. At some level, the songs being made can be peculiar based on music technique or lyrics but as uniquely as each song is, they each tell a story that reflects on the band, especially towards Freddie Mercury. The members work together with a start of simple beats and eventually build their way to record-hit music that has helped them raise into fame. Although their music can be at times risky to play and showcase to the world whether its the music's story concept but that has never stopped them. From "We are the Champions" to "We Will Rock You", it has already became a worldwide sensation, especially when played in sport stadiums. Their goal to create such music was to have their audience to interact and be a part of the show and eventually to our hearts.
