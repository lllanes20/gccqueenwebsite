Bohemian Rhapsody: The Movie

Film Summary

The movie, Bohemian Rhapsody, is a PG-13 film by Sony Pictures that was released on November 2, 2018. It has a runtime of 2 hours and 15 minutes. It is based on the infamous band, Queen and the story follows the leader,Freddie Mercury into his journey prior to the formation of the band. He learns that the vocalist of Smile has to quit, offering Freddie the opportunity to join and create of what is now Queen. Throughout his career along with his fellow band members, Brian, Roger, and John, we take closer look of how the members make music and face through ups and downs in order to reach to fame. Aside from their successful career, Freddie faces battle of acceptance in his personal life in regards to his family, fiance, intimate relationships, and his sexuality. According to IMDB, the film has a 7.9 out of 10 star rating. According to Rotten Tomatoes, the film made $216.3 million in Box Office.
