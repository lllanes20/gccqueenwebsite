Queen's Major Live Performances

Queen vs The World

Since the rise of popularity of Queen, the band has become a great influence to rock and pop culture. With the release of their album, "Queen", they raised major popularity in Britain and eventually earned a tour that expanded to even the United States as guests to Mott the Hoople, but that was put to a halt due to Brian's unexpected health concern. Until January of 1975, Queen officialy headed out to the United States for their first major 'international' tour.
